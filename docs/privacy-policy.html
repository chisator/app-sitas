Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SITAS Fitness Center (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the SITAS mobile application (“the App”).

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password used for authentication (managed securely via Supabase Auth).
  • Profile Information: Your name and assigned user role (e.g., member, athlete).
  • Reservation Data: Gym class reservations and your booking history.
  • Workout Logs: Manually entered training data including assigned routines, exercises, sets, repetitions, and weights.
We do NOT collect: Device location data, data from Apple HealthKit, Google Fit, or any other health/biometric sensors. All fitness and health data in the App is manually entered by you.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your login session.
  • Display your gym class reservations and booking history.
  • Store and display your workout logs and training progress.
  • Provide the core functionality of the SITAS Fitness Center app.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the App's functionality.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, a SOC 2 Type 2 compliant database platform. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Authentication is handled via Supabase Auth with secure token storage on your device using Expo SecureStore.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your data is processed by Supabase solely for the purpose of hosting and securing our application's database and authentication services. Supabase acts as a data processor under our instructions.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable).

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below. Account deletion requests will be processed within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete such information.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
